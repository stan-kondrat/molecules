Perfectly elastic collision

The conservation of the total momentum (`vec p = m * vec v`) before and after the collision:

`m_A * vec u_A + m_B * vec v_B = m_A * vec v_A + m_B * vec v_B`

where `vec u` the velocity before collision, and `vec v` the velocity after collision.

The conservation of the total kinetic energy (`E_k = (m * v^2)/2`):

`(m_A * u_A^2)/2 + (m_B * v_B^2)/2 = (m_A * v_A^2)/2 + (m_B * v_B^2)/2`
